About us

Abaza Glass. is one of Egypt’s leading providers of architectural and custom glass solutions. Founded in 2001, we’ve grown from a small workshop to a full-scale production and installation company serving clients across the country.

We work with homeowners, architects, engineers, and businesses to deliver premium glass installations— from stylish home interiors to high-rise facades and skylights. Our projects are known for their durability, modern aesthetics, and environmental efficiency.
