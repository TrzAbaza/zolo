Our Products

Clear Glass

A transparent, colorless glass widely used in windows, doors, and other architectural applications.

Tempered Glass

A type of glass that has been treated to increase its strength and safety compared to standard annealed glass.

Laminated Glass

Constructed of two plies of glass which are bonded together with interlayers to form a permanent bond.

Frosted Glass

Produced by the sandblasting or acid etching of clear sheet glass.

Tinted Glass

Produced by adding metal oxides to the glass during manufacturing.

Double-Glazed Glass

Consists of two panes of glass with a sealed space between them, usually filled with air or a gas like argon.

Low-E Glass

A type of energy-efficient glass that has a thin, transparent coating.
